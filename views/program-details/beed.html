Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEEd)

Train to become an inspiring educator for young minds.

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEEd) program at Talisay City College is designed to prepare students to become professional teachers at the elementary level. The program provides a strong foundation in educational theories and teaching methodologies, with a special focus on child development and inclusive education.

​Talisay City College (TCC) was established in 2004 by the local government of Talisay City, Cebu, to provide quality and affordable tertiary education to its residents. The college initially focused on offering bachelor's degree programs in fields such as Elementary Education, Secondary Education, Industrial Technology, and Hospitality Management.
