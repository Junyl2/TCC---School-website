Our Services

Empowering students with supportive services at every step of their academic journey.

Library Services

Access textbooks, research materials, and digital archives. Study in a quiet, resource-rich environment.

Student Portal

Log in to view grades, schedules, academic records, and announcements via our centralized portal.

Scholarship Assistance

Apply for academic and financial scholarships, with dedicated support for documentation and deadlines.

Guidance & Counseling

We offer academic advising, personal counseling, and career guidance to support your mental well-being.

Medical & Dental Services

On-campus clinic for basic health check-ups, first aid, and referrals, ensuring student safety.

Registrar’s Office

For enrollment, transcript requests, graduation clearance, and official student documents.

ABOUT US

​Talisay City College (TCC) was established in 2004 by the local government of Talisay City, Cebu, to provide quality and affordable tertiary education to its residents.
