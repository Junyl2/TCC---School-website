TCC Enrollments

Explore, learn, and grow in TCC!

Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEEd)

Train to become a licensed elementary school teacher.

Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSEd)

Specialize in teaching high school students in key subjects.

BS in Industrial Technology (BSInTech)

Hands-on training in electronics and computer tech.

BS in Hospitality Management (BSHM)

Prepare for careers in hotels, tourism, and food service.

BS in Hotel, Restaurant, and Tourism Management (BSHRTM)

Hospitality meets tourism in this dynamic dual-focused course.

Diploma in Professional Education (DPE)

Earn your education units to qualify for LET.

ABOUT US

​Talisay City College (TCC) was established in 2004 by the local government of Talisay City, Cebu, to provide quality and affordable tertiary education to its residents.
