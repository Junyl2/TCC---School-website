Admissions

Start your academic journey at Talisay City College.

Requirements

  • Completed Application Form
  • High School Report Card (Form 138)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Birth Certificate (PSA copy)
  • 2x2 ID Pictures (2 copies)
  • Entrance Exam Result (if applicable)

Enrollment Process

Step 1: Download and fill out the application form.
Step 2: Prepare all required documents and visit the TCC Admissions Office.
Step 3: Submit documents and take the entrance exam (if required).
Step 4: Wait for the results and confirmation of enrollment.
Step 5: Proceed to payment and attend the orientation session.

​Talisay City College (TCC) was established in 2004 by the local government of Talisay City, Cebu, to provide quality and affordable tertiary education to its residents. The college initially focused on offering bachelor's degree programs in fields such as Elementary Education, Secondary Education, Industrial Technology, and Hospitality Management.

​Talisay City College (TCC) was established in 2004 by the local government of Talisay City, Cebu, to provide quality and affordable tertiary education to its residents. The college initially focused on offering bachelor's degree programs in fields such as Elementary Education, Secondary Education, Industrial Technology, and Hospitality Management.
